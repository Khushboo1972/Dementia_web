Our Story

Shallion Dementia Support was founded in 2018 by a group of healthcare professionals and family caregivers who recognized the growing need for personalized, compassionate support for individuals living with dementia.

After seeing the challenges faced by families navigating dementia care, we created a platform that bridges the gap between those who need support and those who want to help. What started as a small community initiative has grown into a trusted support network serving hundreds of families across the region.

Today, we're proud to have built a community of dedicated volunteers and supported numerous families through their dementia journey, always guided by our core values of compassion, safety, and dignity.
