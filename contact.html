Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

How quickly will I receive a response?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please call our support line.

Is there a cost for your services?

Our core support services are free for clients. Some specialized services may have associated costs, which will be clearly communicated upfront.

How do I become a volunteer?

Visit our volunteer registration page to apply. All volunteers undergo PVG checks and receive comprehensive training before being matched with clients.

What areas do you serve?

We currently serve the Edinburgh and surrounding areas. We're expanding our services and hope to serve more regions soon.

Can I refer someone for support?

Yes, you can refer a friend or family member. They will need to complete the registration process themselves for consent and privacy reasons.

How are volunteers matched with clients?

We use a careful matching process based on location, availability, interests, and specific needs to ensure compatible and meaningful connections.
