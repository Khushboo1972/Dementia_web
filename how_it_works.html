How Our Dementia Support App Works

Connecting clients living with dementia to caring volunteers is simple, safe, and effective. Follow these steps to get started:

1. Sign Up

Clients: Create a profile including your care needs, location, and preferred support times.

Volunteers: Sign up and share your availability, skills, and interests in dementia care.

2. Profile Matching

Our smart matching system connects clients with volunteers who fit their needs, location, and schedule.

You’ll see suggested matches in your dashboard and can review volunteer profiles or client requests safely.

3. Connect & Communicate

Once a match is confirmed, you can communicate securely through our platform to arrange visits, calls, or activities.

4. Support & Care

Volunteers provide companionship, cognitive engagement, or practical help. Clients receive the support they need, while volunteers gain meaningful experiences.

5. Feedback & Improvement

After each interaction, clients and volunteers can provide feedback. This helps improve future matches and ensures a safe, trustworthy community.
